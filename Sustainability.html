Sustainability

At Osa Dreams we cling to our three pillars; economic growth, social welfare and care for the environment, committing ourselves to present and future generations to take care of the flora and fauna of the Osa Peninsula.

Our ally in this process is the company DONATAPA, a non-profit company that provides its services to companies committed to the environment.
